The curriculum

Nine modules. You choose the mix.

Select up to four modules per full day, tailored to your team in a complimentary one-hour consultation.

1. Human Behaviour

Studies on motivation and culture, and how they shape the way a crew works together.

2. Human Limitations

The effects of fatigue, stress and illusion on real-world performance.

3. Leadership & Followership

Command principles, delegation and when to lead versus when to follow.

4. Decision Making

Conscious and unconscious decision processes, and how to improve both.

5. Critique

Delivering and receiving feedback in a way that lands and holds.

6. Teamwork & Team Building

Flow, bonding and the habits that turn a group into a crew.

7. Mission Planning & Execution

Military-grade planning approaches adapted to your operation.

8. Situational Awareness

Detecting when you have lost the big picture, and regaining it fast.

9. Threat & Error Management

Safety management systems and practical risk tools for the front line.
