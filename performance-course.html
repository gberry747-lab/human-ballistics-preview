Why it exists

Standard training teaches the aircraft. This teaches you.

Created in 2010, the Performance Course fills the gap that traditional CRM and technical training leave open. It does not try to remove pressure. It changes your reaction to it, using a new paradigm and coaching that rapidly amplifies situational awareness.

🧠

Understand the reaction

See exactly what stress and startle do to attention, memory and decision making, and why willpower alone does not fix it.

⚖

Resolve goal conflict

Learn where the pressure actually comes from: the collision of competing obligations under time constraint. Name it and you can manage it.

🔨

Build practical tools

Walk away with repeatable techniques you can use in the sim, on the line, in the exam room and in life.

The core idea in one picture

Find your amber zone.

A little pressure sharpens you. Too much and performance falls away into anxiety, exactly what happens in a tough check. The goal is not zero pressure. It is learning to work in the amber zone, close to your true ability, when the stakes are highest.

This is the model at the heart of the course, and the tools to stay in that zone are what you take home.

What you learn

The two-day arc.

The science of the moment

What happens in your body and brain when a failure lands or an examiner watches, backed by real stories and the research behind them.

Building resilience to startle

Techniques to shorten the startle response and keep it from cascading into a degraded performance.

Presence of mind under load

Practical methods to hold composure and keep your full faculties available when it counts.

Regaining situational awareness

How to notice you have lost the big picture and rebuild it quickly, before small problems become large ones.

Carrying it home

Many past participants report the tools improved not only their checks, but their family life and wellbeing too.

Good to know

Questions people ask.

Is this only for pilots?

No. It began in aviation and most participants are pilots, but the method works for anyone judged under pressure: doctors, police, surgeons, mining professionals and leaders. The examples are drawn from many fields.

I have a check ride coming up. Will this help in time?

Yes. Many participants attend specifically before a check or return to line flying. If your date is very close, ask about one-on-one coaching as well.

What do I need to bring?

Just yourself and an open mind. Part of the value is the chance to be honest among peers. All materials are provided.

Can you run a course for my airline or organisation?

Yes. In-house courses and team programs are available. See CRM and Human Factors or get in touch to tailor a program.
