Fear of flying, handled

Fly Calm.

A fear of flying can shrink your world, keep you from family, holidays and opportunities. Fly Calm is a short, private program led by a career pilot who knows exactly how safe modern flying is, and how to help your mind believe it too.

Book your first session How it works

This can be you: relaxed, at ease, and enjoying the view.

The program

Three sessions. One hour each.

Delivered one on one, in person or over Zoom, at a pace that suits you.

✈

1. Education

Understand what actually keeps you safe: aircraft structural integrity, engine redundancy and the standards pilots are trained and tested to. Knowledge dissolves a surprising amount of fear.

🧠

2. Mental skills

Learn how associative memory works, and how past fears can quietly trigger anxiety today. Once you see the mechanism, you can interrupt it.

🌴

3. Presence of mind

Practise three practical methods to stay composed and manage a fear or stress response, on the ground and in the air.
